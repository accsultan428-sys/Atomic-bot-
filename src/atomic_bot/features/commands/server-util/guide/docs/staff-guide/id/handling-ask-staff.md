## Handling Ask Staff Tickets
The Ask Staff channel is the go-to place for members and buyers with general questions. When a ticket comes in, here's what you do.

---

1. When you receive an Ask Staff notification, press the Answer button to claim the ticket.
2. A dedicated thread will be created automatically. Begin assisting the member inside that thread.
3. Respond professionally and informatively. Use language the member can easily understand — avoid unnecessary jargon.
4. If the issue cannot be resolved at your level, escalate it to a PR Associate or the appropriate division — do not leave the member without a response.
5. Once the member's question is fully addressed and there are no remaining concerns, close the ticket.
